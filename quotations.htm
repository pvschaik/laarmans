  16. Quality, cost and more
    a. "Als je op de kosten let gaat de kwaliteit omlaag. Als je op de kwaliteit let gaan de kosten omlaag."
    ("If you pay attention to costs then quality goes down. If you pay attention to quality costs go down.")
    (Wim van der Meeren, CEO CZ Health Insurance, 2010 AD)
    b. "If you keep your eye on the profit, you're going to skimp on the product. But if you focus on making really great products, then the profits will follow." (Steve Jobs, no date)
    c. The three-variable problems of quality, costs and time (Alan Jones [SCM], approximately 2003)
    c1 "If high quality and low cost are required then turn-around will not be fast/will be slow."
    c2 "If high quality and fast turn-around are required then it will not be cheap/will be costly."
    c3 "If low cost and fast turn-around are required then quality will be poor."
    d. "If you pay peanuts (in other words, low cost) you get monkeys (in other words, low quality)." (1966 L. Coulthard in Director Aug. 228, according to https://uk.answers.yahoo.com/question/index?qid=20130225050952AAwzAbK)
    e. "Let bij een aanbesteding niet op de kosten, maar op tijdigheid en kwaliteit."
    ("In tendering, do not pay attention to costs, but to timeliness and quality.")
    (Automatiseerder: we geven de regie te snel uit handen en IT'ers luisteren slecht naar de klant. Trouw, 15/10/2014)
